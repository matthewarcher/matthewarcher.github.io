I am a physical oceanographer in the Coastal and Regional Oceanography Lab at UNSW, working with Moninya Roughan and Shane Keating. My research focuses on mesoscale and submesoscale ocean current variability. At UNSW I work with a variety of observational platforms to investigate the structure and variability of the East Australian Current and its eddy field, and how it influences the shelf waters of eastern Australia. I recently managed the deployment of two new HF radar sites in New South Wales, and currently teach the Physical Oceanography component in a Master's course at the Sydney Institute of Marine Science.
